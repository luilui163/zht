Capital asset pricing model(CAPM)

The CAPM allows investors to quantify the expected return on investment given the investment risk,risk-free rate of return,expected market return and beta of an asset or portfolio.The formula used in CAPM is:

$$ E[r_i]=r_f+\beta_i(E[r_M]-r_f) $$
